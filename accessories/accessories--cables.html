Accessories: Cables

Musicians need good cables! Ordinary cables lose much of the signal in transmission and results in glitches and mutes. Good cables improve the quality of a setup by preventing any loss of signals. A guitar player must invest wisely while purchasing guitar cables and pick the cables that suit the style of his music.
